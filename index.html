At Maridadi Glass and Hardware, we pride ourselves on being your premier destination for top-quality construction materials in Wote Town, Makueni County. We specialize in providing a comprehensive range of products to meet all your building needs, from durable glass solutions to essential hardware supplies.

Our extensive inventory includes high-grade glass, robust construction tools and a variety of other essential materials that ensure your projects are completed with precision and durability. With a commitment to exceptional customer service and competitive pricing, Maridadi Glass and Hardware is dedicated to supporting both professional builders and Do It Yourself (DIY) enthusiasts alike.

Whether you’re working on a large-scale construction project or a small home renovation, our knowledgeable staff is here to offer expert advice and help you find the right products for your needs. Visit us in Wote Town in either of our shops opposite Marikiti market or at Double K plaza Shop No. 7 and experience the difference of working with a trusted local supplier who understands the importance of quality and reliability in every build.
